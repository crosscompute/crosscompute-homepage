Bring Your Reports and Articles to Life!

Benefits

  • Automate your reports as embeddable widgets, dashboards, PDF documents or interactive notebooks.
  • Define a schedule for your tables, charts, maps to update themselves.

  • News Organizations: Help readers connect with services in their community by embedding tables, charts, maps that are personalized to your reader's location and demographic
  • Technical Publishing Companies: Provide interactive educational exercises for your readers
  • Electrical Equipment Manufacturers: Provide interactive decision support tools to help your customers decide which product fits their needs
  • Government Agencies and Trade Associations: Save time and money by automating routine reports
  • Power Utilities: Generate living infrastructure maps that communicate planned upgrades and service disruptions
